Triangles

A triangle is a 3-sided polygon sometimes called the trigon. Every triangle has three sides and three angles, some of which may be the same.

The common types of triangles are equilateral, isosceles and scalene.

Equilateral triangle

The equilateral triangle is a triangle with all sides equal and all angles equal

Isosceles triangle

An isosceles triangle is a triangle with two sides equal and two angles equal

Scalene triangle

A scalene triangle is a triangle that all its three sides have different length and different angles.
